Bratwurst

Grilled bratwursts by the dozen.

Bratwurst is a type of German sausage made from veal, beef, or most commonly pork.

he name is derived from the Old High German Brätwurst, from brät-, finely chopped meat, and Wurst, sausage, although in modern German it is often associated with the verb braten, to pan fry or roast.[

Beef and veal are usually incorporated amongst a blend often including pork.

Ingrediends

  • 1-5 bratwursts, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Buy bratwurst at you local (german) buchery
  2. Put the bratwursts on a grill or in a frypan
  3. Fry until they look irresistible
  4. »Mangiare Bratwurst e poi muori!«
